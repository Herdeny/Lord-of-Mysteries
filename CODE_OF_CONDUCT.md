# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic
status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or
sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people;
- respecting differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences;
- giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback;
- accepting responsibility, apologizing to those affected by mistakes, and
  learning from the experience;
- focusing on what is best for the community and project.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- sexualized language or imagery and sexual attention or advances;
- trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks;
- public or private harassment;
- publishing others' private information without explicit permission;
- copyright infringement, plagiarism, impersonation, or deliberate
  misrepresentation of authorship;
- other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these
standards and may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits,
issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code applies within project spaces and when an individual officially
represents the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Report abusive or unacceptable behavior privately to a maintainer through the
contact options on their GitHub profile. Do not include sensitive personal
information in a public Issue. All complaints will be reviewed promptly and
fairly, and reporters' privacy and safety will be respected as far as possible.

Maintainers will use the Contributor Covenant enforcement ladder: correction,
warning, temporary ban, or permanent ban, according to impact and pattern.
